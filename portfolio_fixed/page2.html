Hello!

Welcome to my world of movement and expression!

I'm Tamar Rosenzveig, a dancer and performer based in the vibrant city of Tel Aviv, Israel. With a journey that spans from the prestigious BatSheva Ensemble Company to the diverse independent dance scene, I've had the privilege of exploring the endless possibilities of movement and creativity.

My dance odyssey began at a young age with the renowned Hora Jerusalem folk dance company, where I discovered the exhilaration of performing on stage and the power of artistic collaboration. From there, my passion for classical ballet took root, leading me to the esteemed halls of the Jerusalem Academy of Music and Dance and the Maslool Program for Professional Dancers in Tel Aviv.

Joining the BatSheva Ensemble Company was a transformative experience—a chance to immerse myself in the groundbreaking works of Ohad Naharin and Gili Navot, and to discover the boundless potential of movement as a form of storytelling and expression.

In recent years, I've been drawn to the dynamic energy of the independent dance scene, collaborating with visionary choreographers like Dana Rutenberg, Ariel Cohen, and Omri Drumlevich. Each project has been a journey of exploration and discovery, pushing the boundaries of my artistry and challenging me to evolve as a performer.

Of course, no journey is without its challenges. In 2022, I faced a setback—a surgery on my meniscus that tested my resilience and determination. But through perseverance and dedication, I embraced the healing process, emerging stronger and more inspired than ever before.

Today, I'm excited to embark on a new chapter as I pursue certification as a Gyrokinesis and Gyrotonic teacher—a chance to share my love of movement with others and to inspire the next generation of dancers and performers.

Thank you for joining me on this incredible journey. Together, let's celebrate the beauty of movement, the power of expression, and the endless possibilities of creativity.

With gratitude,

Tamar
